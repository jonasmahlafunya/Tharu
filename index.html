About Us

Serving Families
with Honour

Tharu Funeral Services was founded in 2020 with a single purpose: to serve the people of Modimolle and surrounding communities with the dignity and care every family deserves during their most difficult hours.

We are a fully registered funeral service provider (FSP No. 51802), committed to making the funeral planning process as seamless and stress-free as possible — with transparent, affordable plans and fast claims processing.

Our team handles everything with compassion — from transportation and caskets to graveyard décor and family vehicles — so you can focus entirely on honouring your loved one.

What We Offer

Complete Funeral
Arrangements

I

Casket & Hearse

Premium caskets across all plans, paired with professional hearse services to transport your loved one with the honour they deserve.

II

Family Transport

Dedicated family vehicles provided across all plans — from 2 cars on standard plans to 4 on premium — ensuring everyone travels together.

III

Ceremony Supplies

Full chair and programme sets, graveyard décor, tents and seating at home, and refreshments — everything needed for a meaningful send-off.

IV

Storage & Preparation

Professional mortuary storage and body preparation with full dignity and care, ensuring your loved one is treated with utmost respect.

V

Grocery & Airtime

Grocery vouchers from R2,000–R2,500 and R150 free airtime included in every plan, to ease the burden on the family in difficult times.

VI

Free Memorial Video

A complimentary memorial video is included with every plan — a lasting tribute to celebrate the life of your loved one.

Affordable Plans
for Every Family
